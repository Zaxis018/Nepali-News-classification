About Us

This is a project developed by the students of Thapathali Campus as their minor project. We got our motivation to develop our project due to the increment of fake news on different social media platform and lack of tools for Nepali text based news.

Fake News

Fake news refers to fabricated or intentionally misleading information presented as if it were real news. This can take many forms, including false stories, rumors, conspiracy theories, and manipulated images or videos.

Clickbait

Clickbait refers to online content like headlines, images or videos that is designed to grab the reader's attention and entice them to click on link to the page. The content often uses sensational language or provocative images to generate interest, even if the content itself is not informative.
